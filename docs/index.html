Privacy

Your data stays yours

Claruss is built with privacy at its core.

What we collect

Your email, display name, and optional phone number for OTP delivery. Your rules and lock history are stored on our servers to sync across sessions.

What we don't collect

We never access your screen time data, app usage statistics, browsing history, or any content on your device. Blocked app tokens are opaque and device-specific.

No data sharing

We do not sell, share, or provide your personal data to third parties. Partners can only see that you requested an unlock — nothing more.

Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. OTP codes are hashed and auto-expire. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

For privacy questions, contact privacy@claruss.app
